About Company

Tropical Homes Ltd. began its journey in 1996. We've completed a route filled with obstacles and delights for the past three decades, gathering extensive expertise along the way. Today, we are one of leading real estate developers with a reputation for identifying emerging real estate markets, recognizing aspirations in lifestyle evolution, perfecting the modulations of luxury and delivering projects that capture the unique spirit of changing Bangladesh.

We've worked hard to understand the nuances of the real estate world, empathizing with the victories and tribulations of landowners, customers, employees, and everyone else involved in our endeavors. Our goal is to delve further into understanding while using our engineering and managerial capabilities for the benefit of our stakeholders and our country.
